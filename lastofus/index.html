Introduction to The Last of Us

The Last of Us is an action-adventure game developed by Naughty Dog. It follows the story of Joel and Ellie as they navigate a post-apocalyptic United States ravaged by a deadly fungal infection. The game received critical acclaim for its storytelling, characters, and gameplay.
